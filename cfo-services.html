Virtual CFO Services

Outsourced corporate finance and accounting functions offering strategic leadership without the overhead of a full-time executive.

Tailored Financial Leadership for Growing Businesses

Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) often reach a growth stage where they require high-level financial strategy but cannot justify the cost of employing a full-time Chief Financial Officer (CFO). GSA bridges this gap by providing an outsourced virtual CFO service. We act as an integral part of your executive team, scaling our involvement based on your business requirements.

Our Onboarding & Delivery Process

We implement a structured onboarding methodology to ensure a smooth transition of your accounting operations:

01

Discovery

Analyzing your current accounting workflows, reporting cycle bottlenecks, and cash management habits.

02

System Setup

Defining KPIs, mapping standard monthly MIS reporting templates, and configuring software access.

03

Execution

Handling daily bookkeeping, payroll runs, cash monitoring, and monthly compliance filing schedules.

04

Strategic Review

Reviewing budgets against actuals, advising on corporate tax plans, and attending board meetings.

Data Security & ISO Alignment

All virtual CFO bookkeeping operations are conducted under highly secure network structures. We access client books using secure Virtual Private Network (VPN) setups directly on their servers. Biometric controls, USB blocks, and encrypted logins prevent unauthorized access.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Virtual CFO, and how does GSA deliver this service?
A Virtual CFO provides the strategic and financial management expertise of a full-time CFO on a flexible, part-time, or outsourced basis. GSA delivers this by assigning a dedicated partner (CA Gaurang Shah or Tukaram Ghogale) supported by our back-office team. We manage your bookkeeping, cash flow, and tax filings, while acting as a senior advisor for board decisions and funding rounds.
How does a Virtual CFO differ from our existing accountant or auditor?
Your standard accountant focuses on historical record-keeping and basic ledger maintenance. A statutory auditor provides an independent retrospect review of compliance. A Virtual CFO is forward-looking: they analyze cash forecasts, plan budgets, evaluate investment opportunities, and design internal risk control systems to grow your business.
Can you work with our existing accounting team?
Yes. GSA can act as a senior supervisory layer, managing your local bookkeepers to ensure accurate reporting and strict compliance, or we can deploy our own team to handle the entire finance and accounts function.
