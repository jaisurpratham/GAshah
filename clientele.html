Our Clientele Focus

We deliver custom-tailored financial accounting and compliance solutions to clients across multiple international industries.

Supporting Growth Across Diverse Sectors

At Gaurang Shah & Associates (GSA), we measure our success by the tangible value we bring to our clients' businesses. We deliver outsourced services, audits, and strategic corporate advice, modifying our approach based on the scale and compliance needs of each sector.

We work closely with the leadership teams and partners of various businesses, offering high-level support as a seamless virtual extension of their own offices.

Sectors and Industries We Serve

Commercial Aviation & Airlines

UAE Market Focus

We provide high-volume offshore accounts processing to leading commercial airlines based in the UAE. Our team handles specialized, high-volume fuel, cargo, engineering, and vendor invoice audits, preventing billing errors and cash leakage.

Core Platforms: Sage, AMOS (Aviation Software)
Compliance Focus: International vendor audits, fuel ledger matching, cargo manifests.

Chartered Accountancy Practices

UK Practices

We partner exclusively with mid-sized accounting practices in the UK. Operating under a strict remote desktop connection (RDP) model, we act as a secure offshore team, preparing statutory year-end files, bookkeeping, VAT filings, and payroll processing.

Core Platforms: Xero, QuickBooks, Iris, BrightPay
Compliance Focus: FRS 102/105, CT600 Corporation Tax, MTD VAT requirements.

Tech Startups & Early-Stage Ventures

Indian Tech Economy

We advise entrepreneurs in India's technology ecosystem. We guide startups through the complexities of DPIIT registration, seed/Series A pitch decks, financial modelling, ESOP compensation pool allocations, and business valuation diagnostics.

Core Platforms: Excel, Zoho, Tally Prime
Compliance Focus: Startup India exemptions, ESOP taxation, Share Valuation certificates.

MSMEs & Trading Corporate Entities

Domestic Corporates

We manage complete compliance and audits for mid-sized domestic trading and manufacturing operations, covering Indian statutory corporate audits, internal controls, GST, and TDS tax filings.

Core Platforms: Tally, SAP, Zoho Books
Compliance Focus: Companies Act 2013, GST, Income Tax audits, TDS returns.
