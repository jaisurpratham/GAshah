Business Restructuring

Improving financial performance and operational workflows through organizational and system reorganizations.

Reorganizing Operations for Efficiency

Market changes, regulatory revisions, or unexpected drops in volume can affect a company's financial margins. GSA provides strategic business restructuring advice to help leadership teams assess their performance, identify underperforming divisions, and restructure workflows. Our goal is to stabilize your financial position, optimize capital structures, and build operational resilience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What indicates that a company requires restructuring?
Common indicators include persistent decline in EBITDA margins, recurring cash flow deficits, breach of debt covenant ratios (e.g., debt-to-equity or interest coverage), high inventory holding periods, or rising employee overhead costs relative to revenue growth.
How does GSA help negotiate with banks during restructuring?
We compile detailed CMA (Credit Monitoring Arrangement) reports, forecast 3-statement projections demonstrating debt service capabilities under revised terms, and represent your case during consortium bank meetings to secure longer tenures or lower interest rates.
Does GSA manage the legal complexities of restructuring?
We analyze the financial, direct tax (e.g., capital gains exemptions), and indirect tax (GST transfer) implications of the restructuring, coordinating with corporate legal counsels to ensure full compliance with the Companies Act 2013.
