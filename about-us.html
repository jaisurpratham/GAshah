About Our Firm

Over 25 years of professional excellence in auditing, financial advisory, and international accounting offshoring.

Who We Are

Gaurang Shah & Associates (GSA) is a registered Chartered Accountancy firm based in Mumbai, India. We serve a global client base by providing expert auditing, accounts outsourcing, and financial advisory services. Driven by a commitment to quality and professional ethics, we act as a secure, long-term partner for mid-sized accounting practices in the UK, SME corporate clients in India, and large commercial operations in the UAE.

Our infrastructure operates under a secured network with high-speed internet and advanced telecom capabilities, ensuring robust data safety controls. GSA's client deliverables are managed by a highly skilled team of qualified accountants, semi-qualified accountants, and finance graduates who maintain continuous quality checks at every level.

Our Core Values

Absolute Integrity

We adhere strictly to ICAI ethical guidelines and international accounting frameworks, maintaining client confidentiality above all.

Technical Excellence

Our operations are managed by qualified specialists thoroughly trained in UK accounting standards (FRS 102/105) and UAE corporate frameworks.

Strict Data Safety

We prioritize data safety through a virtual extended office model, accessing files on your secure remote desktops without data extraction.

Commitment to Timelines

We operate on defined service-level agreements (SLAs), guaranteeing on-time bookkeeping updates, VAT filings, and statutory reports.

IT Secured Infrastructure

GSA operates under a secure environment. Our infrastructure incorporates several layers of protection to shield client operations:

  • Remote Connections: All offshore staff access client folders via secure Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) and Remote Desktop Protocol (RDP) directly on client servers. No client records are stored on local desks.
  • Terminal Controls: Our local workstations are configured to block external drives (USB locks), file uploads, and unauthorized downloads.
  • Network Protection: Firewalls, secure Wi-Fi connections, and real-time antivirus scans secure our office network.
  • Physical Safety: Workspaces are locked with biometric access controls, and screens are monitored to guarantee complete compliance.
