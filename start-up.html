Frequently Asked Questions

What are the tax benefits of DPIIT Startup India registration?
DPIIT-certified startups can apply for a 3-year income tax holiday under Section 80-IAC of the Income Tax Act. They are also eligible for exemptions from "Angel Tax" on share premium issues under Section 56(2)(viib), simplified compliance self-declarations, and fast-tracked patent applications.
How does GSA assist startups in equity and ESOP planning?
We help founders structure their cap tables to prevent premature dilution. Our team drafts employee stock option plans (ESOPs) that specify vesting schedules, cliff periods, and exercise prices, ensuring tax compliance for both the company and the employees.
Why does a startup need a registered valuer's report?
Under Section 56(2)(viib) and Section 62 of the Companies Act 2013, Indian private limited companies issuing shares at a premium must justify their share price through a valuation report compiled by a IBBI-registered valuer. We coordinate and prepare the underlying models to ensure compliance.
