Services for UAE Corporate Clients

Specialized aviation back-office accounting solutions supporting leading airlines in the Gulf region.

Specialized Aviation & Commercial Outsourcing

Corporate accounting in the Gulf region requires meticulous attention to operational detail, tax compliance, and industry-specific software integration. GSA has developed a specialized niche in providing offshore back-office accounting services to the commercial aviation sector. We currently support **two leading commercial airlines in the UAE** with high-volume invoice processing and revenue reconciliations.

Our team is highly proficient in key enterprise accounting software, including **Sage** and **AMOS** (Aviation Maintenance Management System), allowing us to handle complex technical reconciliations with absolute accuracy.

Workstation Security & SLA Control

Our aviation back-office team operates under secure conditions. We implement IP-based login restrictions, block external USB drives, and strictly prohibit local downloads of passenger or vendor transaction records.

Frequently Asked Questions

What experience does GSA have in airline-specific systems?
We have a dedicated back-office team specialized in aviation accounting. We are highly proficient in **AMOS** (used for tracking aircraft maintenance, components, and parts inventory) and **Sage** (for corporate ledgers), enabling us to audit complex maintenance and engineering invoices.
Why is fuel invoice reconciliation crucial for airlines, and how does GSA help?
Aviation fuel is a major operational expense. Billing errors, incorrect regional taxes, or route discrepancies are common. GSA audits every fuel bill against physical receipts, flight fuel logs, and actual distance flown, ensuring you pay only for fuel actually delivered.
How do you support India-centric operations for UAE airlines?
Gulf carriers operating in India must manage complex GST input tax credits. GSA reconciles your passenger bookings and local purchase records with GSTR-2B data, preventing double-taxation and optimizing your input tax claims.
