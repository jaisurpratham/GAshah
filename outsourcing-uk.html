Services for UK Accounting Practices

Secure, reliable, and cost-effective offshore accounting services designed specifically to support UK-based practices.

A Trustworthy Extension for Your UK Practice

UK-based accountancy practices face continuous pressure to manage client deliverables while controlling overheads relating to office seats, local hiring, and staff training. GSA acts as a secure, dedicated back-office extension, managing standard compliance workloads so your local team can focus on client relationships and high-value advisory operations.

Our founder and senior operational managers have over 20 years of experience in UK accounts offshoring. We closely understand the transition from manual ledger processing to fully digital workflows, integrating seamlessly with major cloud accounting platforms used in the UK market.

Our Process for UK Practices

We implement a structured onboarding methodology to ensure a smooth transition of your accounting operations:

01

03

Frequently Asked Questions

What software programs do you use for UK accounting?
Our operators are highly proficient in all major UK accounting software packages, including Xero, QuickBooks Online, Sage, Iris, BrightPay (for payroll), and TaxCalc. We operate directly on your practice licenses.
How do you ensure the quality and accuracy of year-end files?
Every year-end file prepared by our junior associates undergoes a mandatory review check-sheet audit by our Head of Operations (AVP Tukaram Ghogale, who led UK accounting operations in a major offshoring group). We guarantee that files are delivered ready for your partner sign-off.
How does GSA handle data safety under GDPR regulations?
We work as your extended virtual office. Our team uses secure remote logins (VPN/RDP) to work directly on your local desktops or cloud servers. Because we do not download or store personal data on Indian systems, data control remains entirely within your IT boundaries, satisfying GDPR requirements.
